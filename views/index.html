Meet Engage

Our platform for Employee Development through managed Community Service Projects.

Engage lets you highlight and develop key professional skills in your employees through participation and delivery of community service projects.

We work with NGOs to structure and define projects that can help participants develop skills like leadership, communication, project management and resilience.

Employees can than use our highly interactive platform to manage and deliver these projects with management and HR oversight. We provide you detailed insights and reporting on how your employees are performing at each step of the project.
